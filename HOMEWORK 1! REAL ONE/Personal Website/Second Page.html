Over The Garden Wall - A Short Summary

"Over The Garden Wall" is a Cartoon Network miniseries about two half-brothers, Wirt and Greg, who become lost in a mystical, foggy forest called the Unknown. To find their way home, they must journey through the forest, befriending a talking bluebird named Beatrice and a reclusive Woodsman, while evading the shadowy Beast that haunts the land.
